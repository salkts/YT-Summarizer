Privacy Policy for YT Summarizer

Last updated: December 18, 2024

Overview

YT Summarizer is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension processes YouTube video content to generate AI-powered summaries while keeping your data secure and private.

Key Privacy Principle: We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. All data stays on your device or goes directly to services you configure.

Data Collection and Usage

What We DON'T Collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing history beyond current YouTube videos
  • Video content or summaries on our servers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Your API keys or credentials

What We DO Process:

  • YouTube Video URLs: Sent directly to Google's Gemini API for analysis
  • Generated Summaries: Stored locally in your browser for caching
  • Settings: API keys and preferences stored locally in Chrome storage
  • Usage Statistics: Time saved tracking stored locally only

Third-Party Services

Google Gemini API

When you generate a summary, the extension sends the YouTube video URL directly to Google's Gemini API using your personal API key. This interaction is governed by Google's Privacy Policy:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Gemini API Terms of Service

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: All summaries, settings, and history are stored locally in your Chrome browser
  • No Cloud Storage: We don't maintain any servers or databases
  • User Control: You can clear all data anytime through the extension's settings

Permissions Explanation

  • Storage: To save your settings and cached summaries locally
  • ActiveTab: To detect when you're on a YouTube video page
  • Scripting: To inject the sidebar interface on YouTube
  • Host Permission (youtube.com): To interact with YouTube pages only

Data Security

  • Your API key is stored locally and never transmitted to our servers
  • All communication with Gemini API uses HTTPS encryption
  • No data is shared with third parties beyond your chosen AI service

Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is stored locally and accessible to you
  • Deletion: Use the "Clear History" feature or uninstall the extension
  • Portability: Your data stays with you locally

Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the extension's interface or GitHub repository.
